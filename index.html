ABOUT

Jason Butler is a slam winning spoken word poet who loves performing at venues and festivals as either headline act, eager open-mic participant, slam entrant or host.

His work spans a plethora of topics from the emotional confessional, the occasional rant, grossly exaggerated anecdotes through to stand up silliness. Also, if asked nicely he can merrily weave an improvised verse from thin air.

Based in Cornwall, he arrived oven-ready on the poetry scene in 2014 after two decades performing as a renowned MC under his alter ego Vegas.

During 2020 he has written and performed over 80 "Poem a day during Lockdown" videos all available on his facebook page, based on current event sor a myriad of weird and wonderful topics suggested by his followers.

To find out the latest info on gigs and for regular new poetry videos go to jasonbutlerpoetry on Facebook.

As well as his poetry performances he's currently frontman for the bands Antimatador and Freshly Squeezed performing on stages all over the UK and beyond.
